About

Glossy started in 2010 with the intent of making languages available to those people interested in communicating in them.

We noticed that many languages are documented by academics, but that these are hardly widely available, and rarely to those people hoping to use them in the field.

Richard Littauer is a student of linguistics, knows his way around endangered and low-population languages, and wants to flex his coding muscle. Simon Vansintjan is a coder and designer who knows his way around websites and mobile applications, and wants to flex his linguistic muscle.

They have made their work freely available to anyone, but are poor students, so it'd be great if you could donate.
