About us:

We're a motivated, on-the-ball, two-man team. We make smartphone applications and websites; fast, quality, and for you. We specialise in social media integration and language resources.

Richard is a bleeding-heart American linguist living in Germany, trying to give back some of himself using the web. Here's his website.

Simon is a Belgian-American entrepreneur and all-around coder living in Edinburgh, trying to do what he can to make both a splash and the ends meet. And here's his.

We're distance workers; all we need is an internet connection, some coffee, and a project. We're always just an email away. Here's some of the projects we're currently working on.

  • Healing Words

    We're working on smartphone applications and web resources for low-resource languages to help aid workers, first responders, and volunteers in areas where the language is a genuine barrier. The plan here is exportable dictionaries of commonly used medical terms; to bring healing words where they're needed.

  • Language Dictionaries

    We're making smartphone dictionaries for low-resource languages. We have experience doing this; Richard was the head of a dictionary for a year and a half for a community of 5,000. We're currently planning dictionaries for a couple of languages, but don't have a solid plan yet.

  • Share the Ledge

    We're planning out a social network site based on one thing; sharing knowledge you have, skills you know, with other people in your direct area. Think couch-surfing, but for metalworking and weaving. Find people in your neighbourhood who really want to help with that DIY project, or who you can show how to rig a hammock at 50ft off the ground. Bring your friends.

  • Tigatag

    This is actually a project of Simon's with another friend, Bruno Panara. Richard helps out by writing stuff, but doesn't get paid. Not much of a deal for Richard, really.
